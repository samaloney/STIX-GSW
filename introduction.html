Introduction¶

Solar Orbiter¶

SOLO is deep space mission study the Sun from close up (~1/3 AU) and from out of the ecliptic.

STIX¶

STIX is a hard X-ray imaging spectrometer which covers the energy range from 4 to 150 keV with 1 keV energy resolution. STIX uses a Fourier-transform imaging technique and provides image to provide imaging on angular scales from 7 to 18 arcsec. For more detailed description of the instrument see the instrument paper [Krucker2020].
